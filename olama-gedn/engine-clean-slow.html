How it works

Three layers of phase precision.

Clean Slow doesn't just stretch audio — it preserves the mathematical relationships between every frequency component, simultaneously.

01

Multi-Resolution STFT

The algorithm analyzes audio at three overlapping window sizes simultaneously — capturing both sharp transients and sustained tonal information at their ideal resolutions. No compromises between time and frequency accuracy.

window_sizes: [256, 1024, 4096] | overlap: 87.5%
02

Phase Vocoder Locking

All frequency bins are phase-locked to prevent the smearing that causes "watery" artifacts. Each bin's phase evolution is predicted and enforced — keeping harmonics coherent even at extreme stretch ratios.

03

Transient Shielding

Detected transients are wrapped in a protection zone that bypasses the stretching algorithm entirely during the critical attack window. Your snare lands exactly on the beat. Your vocal consonants stay sharp.

Common questions

What makes Clean Slow different from my DAW's "Complex Pro" mode?
Most DAW stock algorithms use a single FFT window size and apply global phase rotation, which causes pre-echo and phase smearing. Clean Slow uses three simultaneously overlapping window sizes, per-bin phase locking, and transient shielding — these are algorithmic differences, not marketing differences. The artifact floor is measurably lower.
Will this work on material at extreme ratios — like 50% speed?
Yes. The phase-locking system is designed for ratios from 0.25× to 4.0×. At extreme ratios, some material will develop naturally occurring artifacts (this is a mathematical limitation of time-frequency duality, not a plugin limitation). Clean Slow minimizes these to their theoretical floor.
I produce at 44.1kHz. Is there a quality difference vs 96kHz?
No audible difference. The algorithm operates in the frequency domain where sample rate affects the analysis bandwidth, not the quality of the processing itself. Clean Slow is validated and tested at 44.1, 48, 96, and 192kHz — all profiles produce the same artifact floor.
Is this just the Clean Slow engine, or do I get all engines?
All five engines (Classic, Clean Slow, Rubber Drag, Stretch Haze, Granular Drift) plus four DAW profiles plus preset export/import are included in one purchase. This page highlights Clean Slow because it's the most technically demanding engine we built.
